About Pixel Motors

Since our establishment, Pixel Motors has proudly served as an authorized Tata service center in Bengaluru, consistently delivering top-tier service and unmatched support to Tata vehicle owners. We have earned a reputation for our exceptional automotive care, combined with a customer-first approach that has always been the cornerstone of our operations.

With the vision of providing reliable, high-quality service for Tata vehicles, Pixel Motors has continuously met this goal through expert maintenance, superior service, and an unwavering focus on customer satisfaction.

Strategically located in prime and convenient areas such as Bellandur, very close to the IT sector on Outer Ring Road, Pixel Motors is at the forefront of enhancing the value of Tata vehicle ownership. We offer comprehensive service solutions designed to ensure your vehicle runs at its best, every time.

Our team of highly skilled technicians is dedicated to meeting the needs of our customers with precision and care. Whether it’s routine maintenance, complex repairs, or diagnostics, we guarantee timely, professional, and friendly service. At Pixel Motors, we are committed to maintaining your Tata vehicle’s peak performance for years to come.
