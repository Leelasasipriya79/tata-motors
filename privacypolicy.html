Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting Pixel Motors' website. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information when you interact with our website, services, or showroom.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., via forms, calls, or emails). We never sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Non-Personal Information We Record

When you browse our website, our system automatically logs:

  • Internet domain (e.g., "example.com")
  • Browser type (e.g., Chrome, Tata Motors)
  • Operating system (e.g., Windows, iOS)
  • Visit details: Date/time, pages viewed, and referral website (if you linked from another site)

Purpose: This data helps us improve website performance and user experience. No individual identities are tracked.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance navigation (e.g., retain form inputs temporarily)
  • Analyze traffic via tools like Google Analytics

Control: You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

4. Voluntary Information Submission

When you:

  • Book a service or test drive
  • Submit an enquiry form
  • Contact us via email/phone
  • Register on our site

We collect only necessary details (e.g., name, email address, phone number, vehicle details) to fulfill your request.

When you voluntarily send us electronic mail or fill out a form, we will keep a record of this information to respond to you. You may visit our site anonymously without providing personal information.

Important: By submitting your personal information and contact details, you authorize Pixel Motors and its representatives to contact you via Call, SMS, Email or WhatsApp regarding our products, services, and offers. This consent overrides any registration for DNC/NDNC (Do Not Call/National Do Not Call registry).

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Process bookings, repairs, or warranty claims
  • Communication: Respond to enquiries and provide service updates
  • Marketing: Contact you about products/offers (with your implied consent through form submission)
  • Compliance: Share data with TATA Motors or insurers only for service purposes

6. DND/NDNC Compliance

By providing your contact details, you consent to receive communications from us, even if registered under the Do Not Disturb (DND) registry. To opt out, email privacy@pixelmotors.com.

7. Email Security

Emails sent to us are not encrypted. Avoid sharing sensitive data (e.g., payment details) via email.

8. External Links

Our website may link to third-party sites (e.g., TATA Motors). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before sharing data.

9. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
